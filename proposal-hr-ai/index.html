About the British School of Outdoor Education

Who we are

The British School of Outdoor Education supports capability building through practical training, digital skills development, employability support, and professional learning offers. Our public-facing programmes include AI for HR and other workforce-focused training designed to help organisations build stronger capability.

Why we matter here

We bring a practical, implementation-focused approach. Our wider international work includes UN-linked initiatives, and our HR AI offer is built to help professionals understand readiness gaps, build confidence, strengthen governance, and move towards applied AI and AI agent adoption in real HR work.
